How to Access Your Tools
  1. Scan QR Code: Open your phone camera and point at any QR code above. Tap the notification that appears.
  2. Bookmark for Easy Access: Once the tool opens, tap your browser menu and select "Add to Home Screen" or "Bookmark."
  3. Use Offline: Tools work without internet after first visit. Perfect for areas with poor connectivity.
  4. Practice Regularly: Spend 5-10 minutes daily with flashcards or reviewing scripts to build mastery.
  5. Share with Colleagues: Show tools to team members and discuss insights during case conferences.
